Upcoming Levels List

The Upcoming Levels List (ULL) is a comprehensive catalogue of upcoming Top 1–100 Extreme Demons projected to be verified and placed on Pointercrate. The list aims to forecast the future of the Demonlist, and also features unrated Extreme Demons that may have qualified for a rating at the time of their creation.
